# Code of Conduct

## The Short Version

Be direct. Be constructive. Critique code and ideas, not people.

## Expected Behavior

- Communicate clearly and technically.
- Accept feedback on your work without taking it personally.
- Give feedback that is specific and actionable.
- Keep discussions on-topic for the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks or insults directed at contributors.
- Sustained off-topic disruption of issues, PRs, or discussions.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and close or lock issues and
pull requests that violate this policy. Repeat or egregious violations may
result in a temporary or permanent ban from the project.

To report a problem, contact the maintainer directly via GitHub.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces: issues, pull requests, discussions,
and any other forum hosted under this repository.
